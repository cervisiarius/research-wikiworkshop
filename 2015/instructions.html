Submission Instructions

Papers should be no more than 8 pages total in length, though shorter submissions are encouraged. The format is the standard double-column ACM Proceedings Style. Additional information about formatting and style files are available at http://www.acm.org/sigs/publications/proceedings-templates

Papers can be submitted at easychair.org/account/signin.cgi?conf=mlg2013 up to the submission deadline.
